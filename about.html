About Us

Who We Are?

We are a small cats shelter based in Southampton, United Kingdom. We are volunteers who want to make a difference in our community by helping the homeless souls.

We love animals, especially cats and we want to find loving homes for these (little) cats.

What We Do?

We created a small shelter (for the moment) where we keep safe, fed and cared for the cats. In the future, we hope to enlarge our shelter and to make it available also for other animals.

With the help of volunteers, veterinarians and sponsors we get ready these cats for a loving family who is ready to adopt and take them home.

Be A Part Of This

We are very happy to welcome you to take part in this wonderful project. All the help is well received.
