Why adopt a cat?

Some reasons to do so...


Save a life

By adopting you give the animal the chance to a new better life.


A large variety

The shelter has a varied selection of animals.


Love and cuddles

The cat will love you unconditionally and it will hug you with their kindness. Cuddles are their specialty.


Stop euthanasia

Adoption can be the main way to decrease the overpopulation of the animals and reducing the mercy killing.


Help and support

The volunteers at the shelter will be happy and kind to guide you in raising your pet.


Encourage the adoptions

By adopting an animal from the shelter, you will become a model for others to do the same.

Core features:

  • Vaccinated
  • Microchipped
  • Neutered
  • No adoption fee
  • Full support and help
  • Two months free pet insurance
