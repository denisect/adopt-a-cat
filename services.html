Services

See What We Offer

  • Free Adoption

    With the help of our sponsors and volunteers, we are able to make the adoption free. If you want to become a sponsor and/or volunteer, please contact us.

  • Vaccination

    The animal wellbeing means a lot to us, that's why we vaccinate the cats before handing over to the new owner.

    The cats receive vaccines for rabies, parvovirus, distemper, and hepatitis.

  • Microchipping

    By microchipping the cat we make sure that we bound a formal connection between the new owner and the animal. The details of both sides are stored in a database.

  • Neutering

    We neuter the cats before they go to the new home because we try to prevent the abandon of undesired litters and to reduce the overpopulation of unwanted animals in shelters.

  • Pet insurance

    We are happy to give you a two months FREE pet insurance, in case something happens to the animal. This was made possible with the help of sponsors.
